Featured product

Evagene.

A specialist platform for clinical genetics, family history and risk-model exploration.

Evagene is a browser-based platform for drawing, managing and analysing family-history and genetics data. It supports pedigree visualisation, structured family-history capture, risk-model exploration, consent-aware workflows and export into specialist genetics tools.

Visit Evagene Discuss a related workflow
Who it is for

Specialist teams working with families, pedigrees and inherited risk.

Evagene is built for clinical and research teams whose work depends on accurate, visualised family-history data — including genetic counsellors, clinical geneticists, multidisciplinary teams and adjacent research workflows.

It replaces the typical mix of hand-drawn pedigrees, scanned forms and ad-hoc spreadsheets with a single structured tool that respects clinical and research data quality.

The workflow it replaces

Hand-drawn pedigrees, scattered spreadsheets, fragile templates.

Family-history data is high-value, repeatedly referenced, and notoriously hard to keep tidy across documents. Evagene gives teams one place to capture it properly, look at it clearly, reason about it cleanly, and pass it on without losing detail.

What Evagene does

Capabilities.

Pedigree visualisation

Intuitive drawing tools and clean, printable family trees that meet the expectations of clinical and research review.

Structured family-history capture

Record individuals, relationships and clinical attributes in a real schema, not as drawings.

Risk-model exploration

Investigate inherited risk in a controlled, reproducible way, with the data and the model side by side.

Consent-aware workflows

Designed for environments where consent and provenance matter, and where the trail must survive staff changes.

Export into specialist tools

Clean handover to downstream genetics software, multidisciplinary review and onward reporting.

Browser-based

No install, no per-machine licensing friction, no managed-device headache.

Integration

Talks cleanly to the rest of the stack.

Evagene treats import, export and integration as first-class. We work with teams to fit it into existing record systems, downstream reporting and adjacent research tools.

Intended use

A workflow and data tool, not a medical device.

Evagene supports clinical and research teams in capturing, visualising and reasoning about family-history information. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis or treatment recommendations. For deployments with specific regulatory considerations, talk to Xiasma about scope and intended-use language.
