How we approach product work

Designed by people who have built specialist software before.

Xiasma's products are built to live inside the daily work of expert teams. We model the domain carefully, design for the people who actually use the tool, and design for export and integration from day one.

Domain first

The data model is the product. We spend time getting the schema right before designing screens.

Real users

Workflows reflect how domain experts actually work, not how a generic enterprise template assumes they should.

Built to last

We treat maintenance, change and evolution as the main event, not an afterthought.

Quietly exportable

Specialist data should travel cleanly. Our products treat export and integration as first-class.
