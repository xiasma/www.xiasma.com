Experience

More than two decades of building software for complex domains.

Xiasma Limited is a UK software development company incorporated in 2002. The company has spent more than two decades building software for complex domains, including life sciences, health, public-sector systems, retail, utilities, finance, education and enterprise operations.

Today, Xiasma creates specialist software products such as Evagene, while also supporting organisations that need careful, technically robust software for unusual workflows and data-heavy environments.

How Xiasma thinks

Principles.

Domain first

Model the workflow before scoping the build. The data model is the product.

Narrow scopes, working software

Pick scopes small enough to do well and ship working software early, so progress is visible and decisions stay informed.

Long stewardship

Xiasma is comfortable staying with a product for years. Maintenance and evolution are treated as the main event.

Honest about scope

We would rather decline than over-promise, and we will say where we are not the right fit.

Accessible by default

Our work targets WCAG 2.2 AA as a baseline, not as an add-on.

Calm and considered

Predictable engagements, plain-language reporting and careful engineering, rather than theatre.
