Featured product

Evagene.

A specialist platform for clinical genetics, family history and risk-model exploration.

Evagene is a browser-based platform for drawing, managing and analysing family-history and genetics data. It supports pedigree visualisation, structured family-history capture, risk-model exploration, consent-aware workflows and export into specialist genetics tools.

Evagene is the modern, public face of Xiasma's product work, and shows how we approach polished, domain-specific software.

Visit Evagene Read more
  • Pedigree visualisation — intuitive drawing with clean, printable output for clinical and research review.
  • Structured family-history capture — record relationships and clinical attributes in a real schema, not as drawings.
  • Risk-model exploration — tools to investigate inherited risk in a controlled, reproducible way.
  • Consent-aware workflows — designed for environments where consent and provenance matter.
  • Export to specialist tools — clean handover to downstream genetics software, reports and reviews.
